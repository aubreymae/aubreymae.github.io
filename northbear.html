North Bear

North Bear is a virtual reality puzzle game set in the Arctic North. The player takes on the role of a polar bear trying to survive in a changing landscape affected by climate change. Working alongside 20+ people, the game was developed as a year-long project for DATT 3700 Collaborative Project Development. As part of the Game Design team, Aubrey liaised with the Art Department and the Programming Department to ensure the overall direction and feel of the game was successfully met. The role also called for creating conceptual designs of game levels, gameplay mechanics, and art direction.

My role
